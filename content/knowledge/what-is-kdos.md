KDOS (Knowledge, Documentation, Ontology System) is a compounding knowledge archive designed to structure, preserve, and interlink understanding across thousands of domains.

Each entry is designed to be:
- Atomic
- Prompt-generatable
- Interlinkable
- Timeless
