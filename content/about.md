## What is KDOS?

KDOS (Knowledge, Documentation, Ontology System) is a structured knowledge base designed to organize information into clear, interconnected domains.

It prioritizes:
- **Clarity**: Concise definitions and explanations.
- **Structure**: Strict taxonomy of domains and tags.
- **Utility**: Focused on learning and reference.

This site is built with Hugo and PaperMod, optimized for speed and readability.
